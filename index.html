Custom GPT Companions

Purpose-built assistants that extend the Attuned Perception Framework into guided reflection, mapping, and analysis.

The Clarity Mirror

Interprets uploaded FEMS maps (image/JSON). Surfaces dominant nodes, resonance vs. dissonance, anchors, and offers gentle reflection prompts. (Not clinical guidance.)

Open GPT

Clarity Mapper Pro

For researchers & practitioners: converts FEMS maps into numeric features (counts, sizes, distances, resonance/dissonance amplitudes, sub‑nodes) with CSV/JSON export for analysis.

Open GPT

CORE Muse

Guides CORE mapping: tag chunks, orient spatially (Chunk–Orient–Relate), and turn the map into an outline or talking points.

Open GPT
