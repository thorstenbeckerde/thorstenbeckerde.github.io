My HTML Applications

A collection of web-based tools for thought, creativity, and strategic planning.

Main Apps under the Clarity Navigation framework

These are the core tools for mapping internal experiences.

  • Fuzzy Emotion Mapping System (FEMS)

    A visual tool for exploring your emotional landscape. Map aspects of your inner world to gain clarity on feelings and their connections.

  • CHORUS

    A non-linear note-taking system for writers and creatives. Structure your thoughts as constellations of ideas, not rigid outlines.

  • Stellary

    A poetic and sensory approach to memory. Create "constellations" of images, sounds, and text fragments to map and reflect on experiences.

Specialized Game & Narrative Design Versions

Derivatives of the main apps, tailored for storytellers and game developers.

  • NPC Emotion Weaver

    A tool for game writers to visually map the emotional states, triggers, and narrative arcs of Non-Player Characters (NPCs).

  • CHORUS Perceptual Storyteller

    A narrative design tool for mapping stories based on character perception and sensory experience. Ideal for interactive fiction, with exports to Ren'Py, Yarn, and Ink.

Professional & Strategic Editions

Tools adapted for business, planning, and analysis.

  • CHORUS Strategist Edition

    A powerful version of CHORUS for strategic planning. Map out objectives, assets, threats, and courses of action for business, wargaming, or competitive analysis.
